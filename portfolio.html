Thos. Moser Cabinetmakers

Consumers purchase Thos. Moser furniture for more than the excellence of the product – they respond to the ethic of hand-crafted quality and workmanship. These ads appeal to that emotional connection.


Copy

Senses

Furniture for all your senses.

Your hand is inexorably drawn to the sumptuous finish. You hear the sure, squeak-free “thunk” of loving craftsmanship. The cherry wood’s aroma enriches your home. Beautiful? Of course. But not for your eyes alone.

Touch

Love at first touch.

Lovingly crafted furniture that nourishes your world. Imagine your hand lingering on its painstakingly hewn cherry frame. Extraordinary to look at, restorative to sit on, inquiring to live with, utterly gratifying to own.

Tree

With care, this chair will outlast the tree it’s made from.

Our American black cherry is meticulously sculpted for elegance and integrity, then hand finished with repeated passes of ultrafine sandpaper, 3 coats of slightly heated linseed oil enrich the finish. This craftsmanship ensures that, with care, Thos. Moser furniture will be cherished by both you and your heirs. After all, cherry is scarce—it would be a crime to make something unworthy of it.

MIT Sloan Management Review

A print execution of a yearlong repositioning campaign, this ad highlights MIT’s unique advantage for business audiences -- academic-level concepts applied to real-world problems.


Copy

40,000 Foot Thinking Meets Ground Level Doing

Traditionally, when it comes to innovation, managers face a yawning chasm between generating big ideas and getting real work accomplished. At MIT Sloan Management Review, we bridge the gap between great thoughts and great results.

Especially now, since we’ve thoroughly re-imagined and re-designed both our print edition and our web site. Even if you’re familiar with MIT SMR, you’ll discover that we are now focused on innovation—leading change, fostering creative thinking, and building an innovative culture. And as you’d expect from MIT, you’ll find innovation that works, that results in a tangible result, that’s a little different from what anyone else is saying or doing.

See for yourself. Visit us at sloanreview.mit.edu. And see what happens when thinking it up meets getting it done
